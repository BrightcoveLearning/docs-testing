{{ site.product }} Known Issues

This topic lists known issues for the {{ site.product }} application.

Advertising

  1. If advertisements are configured at the playlist level, when a video from that playlist is found and played via Search, Recently Watched, Related or Favorites, the adds will not play. Noe that if an ad was assigned to the video directly, that ad will still play.

Parental controls

  1. All content is displayed to end users (in lists, carousels, etc), but when trying to play the video the parental pin will be requested.
